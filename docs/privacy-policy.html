UniPass

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2025

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our peer-to-peer social networking app.

Information We Collect

When you use the app, we collect the following information:

  • Your profile data: name, photo, bio, hometown, field of study (and year), and tags
  • Peer interactions: UUIDs of nearby users and the date/time of interactions
  • Meetup data: title, description, location, date, and participants (UUIDs)

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to enable social connections and interactions between nearby users, and to facilitate meetups organized through the app.

Data Storage

All data is stored in Apple's CloudKit. At this stage of development, data is not encrypted at rest in a private database. This means your profile and interaction data may be accessible in plain form within the database.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Your profile is only visible to other users when you come into proximity and exchange UUIDs via peer-to-peer communication.

Your Rights

You can request that your data be deleted or modified at any time. To do so, contact us at the email below.
