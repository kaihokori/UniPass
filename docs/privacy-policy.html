Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 24, 2025

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our peer-to-peer social networking app.

Information We Collect

When you use the app, we collect the following information:

  • Your profile data: name, photo, bio, hometown, field of study (and year), and tags
  • Peer interactions: UUIDs of nearby users and the date/time of interactions
  • Meetup data: title, description, location, date, and participants (UUIDs)

How We Use Your Information

Your data is used to enable social connections and interactions between nearby users, and to facilitate meetups organized through the app.

Data Storage

All user data is stored in Apple's CloudKit service. CloudKit encrypts data both in transit and at rest, using Appleâ€™s standard security practices. While some profile data is stored in a public database to enable social discovery, access is limited to users of the app and is not shared with third parties.

Security

We rely on Apple's CloudKit infrastructure to securely store and transmit your data. Apple encrypts data at rest and during transmission using industry-standard protocols. At this time, we do not implement additional encryption or cryptographic algorithms within the app.

Sharing of Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Only your UUID is exchanged via peer-to-peer discovery. Profile information is then fetched securely from CloudKit using that UUID. No sensitive or identifying information is transmitted directly over local connections.

Your Rights

You can request that your data be deleted or modified at any time. To do so, contact us at the email below.
